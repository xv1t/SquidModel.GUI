Option Name:htcp_port
Replaces:
Requires:--enable-htcp
Default Value:htcp_port 4827
Suggested Config: 


	The port number where Squid sends and receives HTCP queries to
	and from neighbor caches.  Default is 4827.  To disable use
	"0".
